About Me

Aspiring Model &
Creative Force

My name is Amna Razak — I am an aspiring model with a passion for fashion, creativity, and visual storytelling. I enjoy being in front of the camera and exploring different styles, expressions, and concepts through photography.

I believe every photoshoot is an opportunity to learn, grow, and create something meaningful. Whether it's a portrait session, editorial concept, lifestyle shoot, or brand collaboration, I approach each project with dedication, professionalism, and a positive attitude.

My goal is to grow as a model, embrace new opportunities, and create images that tell a story while leaving a lasting impression.
